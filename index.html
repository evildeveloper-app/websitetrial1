FAQ

The questions that actually matter.

Can you see my transactions?+
No. There's no Lucid server and no database. Your data is stored only on your device or in a Google Sheet inside your own Google account. We have no way to see it.
How do you make money, then?+
Only from the optional Lucid Pro upgrade — never from your data. No ads, no selling information, no hidden data deals. If you never upgrade, Lucid stays free forever.
Do I have to link my bank login?+
Never. Lucid doesn't ask for bank passwords. You add transactions yourself, or import a file your bank lets you download. Your banking credentials never touch Lucid.
What happens if I stop using it?+
Nothing is held hostage. You can export everything, or delete it, in one tap. Because the data is in your own account, it's always yours to take with you.
Is it really free?+
Yes. The core app — tracking, budgets, goals, family sharing, bank import — is free forever. Pro is an optional upgrade for AI features and instant sync.
